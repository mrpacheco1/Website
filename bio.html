I am web designer, instructor and soccer coach in brooklyn, NY. I have over 5 years teaching computer systems, and over 10 years working in the IT field. There is nothing I enjoy the most than seeing my students succeed in their studies and personal life. Currently I teach web design and web development at Opportunities for a Better Tomorrow in Bushwick Brooklyn.I also coach a soccer team & I am a photographer during my spare time.

Computers are my life, I started at a very young age with my home computer, most of my skills and knowledge are self taught. In my current position as Senior Technoloty Instructor at Opportunities for a Better Tomorrow my priority has been to implement new ways to engage the disconnected youth through technology. I have built many curricula to reach out too the disconnected youth among them are the web design and coding fundamental program. This program teaches young adults how to design, develop and code a website.
